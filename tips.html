Smart maintenance

Practical articles about cleaning, organization and care.

Remove odors from upholstery

Baking soda, let it sit for 30 minutes and vacuum. For strong odors, professional steam is the solution.

Ideal frequency for carpets

In homes with pets or children, deep clean every 6 months. Weekly vacuuming extends lifespan.

Ventilation after cleaning

Open doors and windows for 20 minutes after using chemicals to reduce humidity and improve air quality.

Order before cleaning

Picking up objects from the floor allows for more efficient cleaning. Spend 5 minutes clearing the area.

Protectors for fabric sofas

Apply anti-moisture spray every 3 months to prevent stains and make daily cleaning easier.

When NOT to use a pressure washer

Avoid on chipped surfaces or unsealed wood. Consult a professional.
